Why we built this

A kinder mirror for your mood.

We combine computer vision with a lightweight wellbeing survey and an OpenAI copilot to surface concise, empathetic reflections—not diagnoses.

  • MTCNN/Haar detection + CLAHE for lighting resilience.
  • AffectNet and CK+ checkpoints prioritized for better label coverage.
  • Short, reverse-scored Likert survey to catch stress drift.
